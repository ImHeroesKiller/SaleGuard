About SaleGuard

SaleGuard is a web application designed to monitor and manage sales activities across various distributors. The system ensures that the products distributed meet company standards and helps identify any variations that may occur.

Features

  • Product Tracking: Monitors the status and conformity of products distributed to various distributors.
  • Real-time Data: Provides real-time sales data for performance analysis.
  • Sales Reports: Generates comprehensive sales reports for management and sales teams.
  • Notifications: Provides automatic notifications if there are discrepancies or issues with distributed products.
  • Interactive Dashboard: Provides an interactive dashboard that facilitates data visualization of sales and product performance.
  • Data Validation: Implements watermark and time/place stamp on photos for data validation.
  • Integration: Integrates with communication platforms like Telegram and WhatsApp for easier coordination and daily operational control.
